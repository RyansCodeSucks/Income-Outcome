# Income & Outcome Calculator 💰

A beautiful, modern web-based calculator to help you track your income, expenses, and see how much money you have available for savings.

## ✨ Features

### 📊 **Income Tracking**
- **Salary/Wages** - Your main employment income
- **Freelance/Part-time** - Additional work income
- **Investment Income** - Dividends, interest, etc.
- **Other Income** - Any other income sources
- **Custom Income Fields** - Add unlimited custom income categories

### 💸 **Expense Tracking**
- **Rent/Mortgage** - Housing costs
- **Utilities** - Electricity, water, internet, etc.
- **Groceries** - Food and household items
- **Transportation** - Gas, public transit, car payments
- **Entertainment** - Movies, dining out, hobbies
- **Healthcare** - Medical expenses, insurance
- **Custom Expense Fields** - Add unlimited custom expense categories

### 🎯 **Smart Calculations**
- **Real-time Updates** - See results as you type
- **Total Income** - Sum of all income sources
- **Total Expenses** - Sum of all expenses
- **Available for Savings** - Money left after expenses
- **Savings Rate** - Percentage of income you can save

### 🏆 **Savings Goals**
- Set monthly savings targets
- Visual feedback on goal achievement
- Track progress over time

### 📈 **History & Analytics**
- Save calculations for future reference
- View calculation history with timestamps
- Track financial trends over time
- Export data (local storage)

## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. **Open the Calculator**
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. No installation required!

### 2. **Enter Your Income**
- Fill in your salary, freelance income, investments, etc.
- Use the "Add Custom Income" button for additional income sources
- All amounts are automatically calculated in real-time

### 3. **Enter Your Expenses**
- Input your monthly expenses in each category
- Use the "Add Custom Expense" button for additional expense categories
- The calculator will show your total expenses

### 4. **View Results**
- Click "Calculate Finances" or see results update automatically
- View your total income, expenses, available money, and savings rate
- Results are displayed in beautiful, easy-to-read cards

### 5. **Set Savings Goals**
- Enter your monthly savings target
- See if you're meeting your goals
- Get visual feedback on your progress

### 6. **Save & Track**
- Save your calculations to track progress over time
- View your calculation history
- Clear history when needed

## 🆘 **Troubleshooting**

### Calculator Not Working?
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Check that all files are in the same folder
- Try refreshing the page

### Results Not Showing?
- Enter at least one income or expense amount
- Check that you're using numbers (not text)
- Try clicking the "Calculate Finances" button

### History Not Saving?
- Ensure your browser supports local storage
- Check that cookies are enabled
- Try using a different browser

## 📞 **Support**
